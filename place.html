Guatemala: Land of Trees

Geography

Guatemala features lush rainforests, volcanoes, and mountains. It is bordered by Mexico, Belize, Honduras, and El Salvador.

Culture

Guatemala is known for its rich Mayan heritage, traditional textiles, and colorful festivals such as Semana Santa.

Demographics

  • Population: ~17 million
  • Capital: Guatemala City
  • Languages: Spanish, 21 Mayan languages

Weather

Guatemala has a tropical climate with distinct wet and dry seasons.
