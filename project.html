Freedom Watch

An awareness initiative against human trafficking

Site Purpose

The goal of Freedom Watch is to inform the public about modern day slavery and human trafficking, provide resources for survivors, and offer guidance on how to get involved in prevention efforts.

Scenarios (Audience Questions)

  • How can I recognize signs of human trafficking in my community?
  • What organizations offer support and rehabilitation for survivors?

Color Schema

  • Dark Charcoal (#2E2E2E) — used for headings, borders, and nav background.
  • Vermilion Red (#E63946) — used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and links.

Typography

  • Montserrat — headings and section titles (font-weight: 700).
  • Open Sans — body text, captions, paragraphs (font-weight: 400, 600).

Testing & Validation

  • HTML validation: W3C Markup Validation
  • CSS validation: W3C CSS Validator
  • Accessibility check: Lighthouse / Axe
  • Color contrast: WebAIM Contrast Checker
  • SEO: Meta tags, descriptive alt text for images
  • Performance: PageSpeed Insights
