What is Human Trafficking?

Human trafficking is modern-day slavery. Victims are exploited through force, fraud, or coercion. Millions around the world are suffering silently.

What You Can Do

  • Recognize the signs of trafficking
  • Report suspicious activity
  • Support survivor-centered organizations
  • Educate your community
