What Is Human Trafficking?

According to the U.S. Department of Justice, human trafficking is the exploitation of a person for labor or commercial sex through force, fraud, or coercion. It includes both sex trafficking and labor trafficking, affecting adults and children around the world. Learn more on the DOJ site.
